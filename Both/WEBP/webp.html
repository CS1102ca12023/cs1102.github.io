Lossy Compression -- WEBP Format

WebP is an image format developed by Google, focus on compress the image on the web, primarily aims to faster the page loading and reduced bandwidth usage.

Advantage Disadvantage
Better Compression: Significantly better compression than JPEG, produce smaller file size and better image quality. Browser Limitation: Not all the browser and tools support WebP.
Support Transparency: WEBP support transparent background not like JPEG, where without overlay white pixel. Duration on En/Decoding: WEBP require more processing power to encode and decode than JPEG, where the calcualation time will be longer on limited computational devices.
Better Compatibility: WEBP support transparent background not like JPEG, where without overlay white pixel. OverCompression: If the ratio of compression is too large, it also will lead to noticable loss of image quality.
