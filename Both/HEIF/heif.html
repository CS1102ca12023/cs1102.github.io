Lossy Compression -- HEIF Format

High Efficiency Image Format (HEIF) is a new image file format recently, they aims to repalce and be an alternative of JPEG where HEIF having better compression method and enhanced image quality.

Advantage Disadvantage
Better Compress Ratio:Allow HEIF have better compression than traditional format like JPEG, mean that it able to store a higher quality of image where less file size. Patnet Fee:HEIF may invloves licensing fee or patents where it chargeable on the compression of using HEVC.
Advanced Features Supported:HEIF support 16-bit color depth, HDR,transparency and mulit-layer image file. Limited Compatibility:Not all the platform and devices fully support HEIF, where may have display error.
